Rules for the Provision of Special Education

Regulations of executive branch agencies are issued by authority of statutes. Like legislation and the Constitution, regulations are a source of primary law in Washington State. The WAC codifies the regulations and arranges them by subject or agency. The online version of the WAC is updated twice a month. Copies of the WAC as they existed each year since 2004 are available in the WAC archive. The OSPI provides additional information regarding special education topics through training and Technical Assistance Papers on discipline, evaluation, and IEPs (follow links, below). Current Rules & Regulations related to Special Education can be found in Chapter 392-172A WAC.
